Animations, Animations, Animations

100Spher.es has a very interesting site setup built around tons of small animations. When the page first load all of the elements on the page transition upwards from their respective locations. The logo also has a continuous animation of a spinning globe. The menu section, denoted by three squiggly lines in the upper left of the page as a hover animation as well. When one puts their mouse over it, the menu pops up via transition from the far left of the screen. Hovering over each element on the screen will cause a small animation as well. Hovering over the pictures causes them to darken, and for small image to appear in the middle. Hovering over the launch icons, located under each picture, causes their colors to invert.
